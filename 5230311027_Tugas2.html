PORSCHE 911 GT3 RS

MAR 26, 2025

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a high-performance sports car that combines racing technology with professional-grade precision design. Designed for automotive lovers who want the purest and most intense driving experience, it represents the pinnacle of Porsche engineering in creating the perfect vehicle for both the street and the race track. With a long history in racing, the 911 GT3 RS is not just a car, but a technical statement of what is possible when technology and passion meet in the automotive arena.

Design

The design of the 911 GT3 RS reflects Porsche's philosophy of creating the perfect the perfect car. Every aerodynamic line is designed with mathematical calculations to improve performance and efficiency. Exclusive carbon fiber and lightweight materials are the main focus in body construction, resulting in a total weight of just 1,490 kg.

Minimalist yet luxurious interiors, with a touch of real racing touches. The carbon fiber steering wheel, special racing seats, and digital screen screen allow for a pure driving experience and directly connected to the engine.

Engine Specification

At the heart of this car is a naturally aspirated 6-cylinder boxer engine with a capacity of 4.0 liters that is capable of producing tremendous power. With technology straight from the racing world, this engine is capable of 518 horsepower at 9,000 rpm, with a maximum torque of 465 Nm. maximum torque of 465 Nm.

Acceleration of 0-62 mph (0-100 km/h) is achieved in just 3.2 seconds, with a maximum speed of 184 mph (296 km/h). Transmission system PDK (Porsche Doppelkupplung) transmission system ensures the quickest and smoothest gear shifts. and smoothest.
